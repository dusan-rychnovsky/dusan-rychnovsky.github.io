Reading Recommendations

Following is a list of my most favorite non-fiction books and blogs, which I can highly recommend to practically anyone. When thoroughly studied and applied, I find they will empower you to drastically improve your life. They are of various different topics, ranging from software engineering (my profession), to personal finance and achieving happiness - which are all areas of life that I am currently most focused on. Some entries contain a link to a document with my notes on the material - feel free to use those in any way.

Blog: Mr. Money Mustache

Link. Peter Adeney will help you realize that true happiness doesn't come from hoarding never ending streams of material possesions at the expense of wasting your life in non-fulfilling corporate jobs, but rather from having the freedom to spend your time doing activities which are meaningful to you and bring you joy. And that for the wast majority for us, financial independence after only a few years in the workforce is perfectly attainable, if we become smart with our expenses and invest the surplus.

My most favourite articles:

  • How Big is your Cirle of Control?
  • The Practical Benefits of Outrageous Optimism
  • The Joy of Part-Time Work
  • Killing your $1000 Grocery Bill
  • A Millionaire is Made Ten Bucks at a Time
  • Living well on the trailing edge of Luxury
